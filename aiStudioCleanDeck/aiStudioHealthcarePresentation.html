Automated Literature Surveillance & Signal Detection

Literature Surveillance

Problem: Manual review of medical literature for adverse event detection is time-consuming.
Solution: Agentic AI autonomously scans literature databases (e.g., PubMed, Embase), extracts relevant adverse event mentions, assesses causality, and prioritizes signals for human review.
Impact: Reduces review time, improves signal detection efficiency, and enhances compliance with regulatory requirements.

Intelligent Case Intake & Triage

Case Intake & Triage

Problem: High volume of Individual Case Safety Reports (ICSRs) from multiple sources leads to delays and errors in case processing.
Solution: Agentic AI automates case intake from emails, forms, and call transcripts, extracts structured data, classifies cases based on severity, and routes them for appropriate processing.
Impact: Faster case processing, improved accuracy, and better resource allocation.

Automated IMDRF & MedDRA Coding

Automated Coding

Problem: Manual coding of adverse events and device malfunctions is labor-intensive and prone to inconsistency.
Solution: AI-powered coding automation maps reported terms to IMDRF/MedDRA codes with high accuracy, learns from human corrections, and self-improves.
Impact: Ensures consistency, speeds up regulatory reporting, and reduces human effort.

Regulatory Intelligence & Compliance Monitoring

Regulatory Intelligence

Problem: Keeping up with evolving global regulatory requirements is challenging for pharmacovigilance and medical device safety teams.
Solution: Agentic AI continuously monitors regulatory websites, extracts and summarizes updates, compares changes, and provides proactive compliance alerts.
Impact: Ensures up-to-date compliance, reduces manual effort, and minimizes regulatory risks.

Automated MDR/FSCA Reporting for Medical Devices

MDR/FSCA Reporting

Problem: Medical device companies struggle with timely and accurate MDR submissions and Field Safety Corrective Actions (FSCA).
Solution: AI automates data extraction from complaint databases, pre-fills MDR forms, validates data against regulatory requirements, and submits reports to authorities.
Impact: Ensures timely compliance, reduces submission errors, and streamlines regulatory reporting.

Real-time Adverse Event Detection from Social Media

Social Media Monitoring

Problem: Traditional pharmacovigilance systems miss real-world safety signals emerging from patient discussions online.
Solution: Agentic AI monitors social media, patient forums, and online reviews, extracts potential safety concerns, and integrates findings into signal detection workflows.
Impact: Enhances real-world evidence collection, identifies emerging risks earlier, and improves drug and device safety.

PV Agreements & SDEA Compliance Automation

PV Agreements & SDEA Compliance

Problem: Ensuring compliance with pharmacovigilance agreements (PVAs) and Safety Data Exchange Agreements (SDEAs) is complex and resource-intensive.
Solution: AI automates agreement tracking, identifies reporting obligations, flags potential non-compliance, and generates reports for audits.
Impact: Reduces compliance risks, saves time, and enhances audit readiness.

Automated Product Label Analysis & Compliance

Product Label Analysis

Problem: Continuous tracking of drug and medical device label updates across different regions is complex, leading to non-compliance risks.
Solution: AI extracts, compares, and analyzes drug/device labels across regulatory agencies (FDA, EMA, MHRA, PMDA, etc.), detects discrepancies, and provides alerts for label updates.
Impact: Ensures label compliance, identifies safety updates proactively, and streamlines label change management.
